About Me




Hi, I'm Vic, the founder of Cubesnack and Cliqpost. My passion lies in creating digital products that have a positive impact on the future. As an optimistic entrepreneur, I constantly seek opportunities to shape the tech industry and make a difference in people's lives. It all started with Cubesnack. I saw a gap in the social and shopping industry, and wanted to provide a platform where that allowed people to shop, sell, and build social connections together on one platform. With dedication and visionary leadership, Cubesnack quickly gained popularity, and it was a fulfilling experience to see my idea become a reality. By prioritizing user experience, I aimed to challenge the status quo and make a difference in the way people engage in the digital space.

For me, being an entrepreneur goes beyond building successful businesses. It's about using innovation as a force for good, uplifting communities, and leaving a lasting, positive impact on society. I strongly believe that technology can be a catalyst for positive social change, and I'm committed to embodying this belief in everything I do. Throughout my journey, I've been fortunate to work with a dedicated team of like-minded individuals who share my passion for building a better future. Together, we strive to push the boundaries of what's possible, always guided by optimism and a collective vision for positive change. Beyond my ventures, I find fulfillment in mentoring aspiring entrepreneurs and participating in industry events to share my experiences and inspire others to pursue their dreams fearlessly. I am driven by the desire to leave a meaningful legacy, and I will continue to use my entrepreneurial spirit and creativity to make a difference in the world. The journey has been challenging, but it's the unwavering optimism and dedication to building products that truly matter that keeps me going.
